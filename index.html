How It Works

Four Steps. No Guesswork.

Most clients never need to set foot in an office. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you sign up.
  1. 1

    Sign up in the client portal

    Create your secure TaxDome account in a minute. You'll get a checklist of exactly what we need based on your situation.

  2. 2

    Upload your documents

    Drag & drop W-2s, 1099s, receipts, QuickBooks exports — or snap photos on your phone. Everything stays encrypted in the portal.

  3. 3

    We prepare and review

    We prepare your return or reconcile your books, message you with any questions inside the portal, and walk you through the results before anything is filed.

  4. 4

    E-sign and we file

    Approve everything with a few taps, we file on your behalf, and you get copies of every document stored permanently in your portal.
